# COPYRIGHT AND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY POLICY

**AI Story Studio - Copyright Protection Framework**

*Last Updated: July 23, 2025*

## 1. COPYRIGHT OVERVIEW

AI Story Studio respects intellectual property rights and operates under strict copyright compliance standards to protect both user content and third-party intellectual property.

## 2. USER CONTENT OWNERSHIP

### 2.1 Original Content Rights
- **User Ownership**: Users retain full ownership of their original creative works
- **Copyright Retention**: All story content, characters, and plots remain user property
- **Moral Rights**: Recognition and integrity rights respected internationally
- **Commercial Rights**: Users may commercialize their content freely

### 2.2 Platform License
- **Limited License**: Users grant AI Story Studio a limited license to:
  - Process and display content within the platform
  - Generate backups and provide synchronization
  - Enable collaboration features when requested
  - Provide technical support and platform functionality

### 2.3 License Limitations
- **No Ownership Transfer**: Platform never claims ownership of user content
- **No Commercial Use**: Platform cannot commercialize user content
- **Revocable License**: Users can revoke license by deleting content
- **Specific Purpose**: License limited to platform functionality only

## 3. AI-GENERATED CONTENT

### 3.1 AI Creation Framework
- **User Direction**: AI generates content based on user prompts and direction
- **Human Creativity**: AI serves as a tool for human creative expression
- **Original Works**: AI-generated content is considered original user work
- **No Pre-existing Claims**: AI does not reproduce copyrighted material

### 3.2 Copyright Ownership
- **User Ownership**: Users own AI-generated content created through the platform
- **Creative Input**: User prompts and direction constitute creative authorship
- **Tool Analogy**: AI functions like a sophisticated creative tool
- **Originality Standard**: Each generation is unique and non-infringing

### 3.3 Quality Assurance
- **Content Screening**: AI trained to avoid reproducing copyrighted works
- **Originality Checks**: Advanced algorithms prevent copyright infringement
- **Similarity Detection**: Monitoring for substantial similarity to existing works
- **User Responsibility**: Users must verify originality of their work

## 4. THIRD-PARTY CONTENT

### 4.1 Prohibited Uses
- **No Infringement**: Users cannot upload copyrighted material without permission
- **No Derivative Works**: Cannot create unauthorized derivatives of copyrighted works
- **No Character Copying**: Cannot use copyrighted characters without license
- **No Trademark Misuse**: Cannot infringe on registered trademarks

### 4.2 Fair Use Guidelines
- **Educational Use**: Limited quotations for educational purposes
- **Commentary**: Brief excerpts for criticism or review
- **Parody Protection**: Transformative parody works may be permitted
- **Attribution Required**: Proper attribution for all quoted material

### 4.3 Permission Requirements
- **Licensing**: Obtain proper licenses for copyrighted material
- **Rights Clearance**: Verify rights for all third-party content
- **Documentation**: Maintain records of permissions and licenses
- **Compliance**: Regular compliance audits and monitoring

## 5. DIGITAL MILLENNIUM COPYRIGHT ACT (DMCA)

### 5.1 DMCA Compliance
- **Safe Harbor**: Platform operates under DMCA safe harbor provisions
- **Agent Designation**: Designated DMCA agent for takedown notices
- **Notice Procedure**: Streamlined process for copyright holders
- **Counter-Notice**: Fair process for users to dispute claims

### 5.2 Takedown Process
1. **Notice Receipt**: Copyright holder submits valid DMCA notice
2. **Content Review**: Platform reviews alleged infringement
3. **Expedited Removal**: Content removed within 24 hours if valid
4. **User Notification**: User notified of takedown and reason
5. **Counter-Notice Option**: User may dispute with counter-notice
6. **Restoration**: Content restored if counter-notice not challenged

### 5.3 Repeat Infringer Policy
- **Three-Strike System**: Progressive enforcement for copyright violations
- **Account Suspension**: Temporary suspension for verified infringement
- **Permanent Termination**: Repeat offenders permanently banned
- **Appeal Process**: Fair review process for disputed violations
